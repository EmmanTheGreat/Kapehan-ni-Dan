About Us

Kapehan ni Dan

Kapehan ni Dan is a web-based platform designed for a coffee shop aiming to enhance customer engagement and drive sales through a unique combination of a social community, loyalty rewards, and e-commerce functionalities. The app allows users to purchase coffee products, share their stories, earn points, and redeem rewards, creating an engaging and interactive experience for coffee enthusiasts.

Coffee of the Day

Americano coffee (Italian: Caffè Americano) is an espresso diluted with hot water. The presence of foam (cream) depends on how coffee and water are mixed. Attention! The concepts of “American” and “American coffee” should not be confused. n the second case, this refers not to diluted espresso, but to a drink prepared through a filter system.
